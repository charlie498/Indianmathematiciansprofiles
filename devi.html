Shakuntala Devi

Born: November 4, 1929, Bangalore, India

Died: April 21, 2013, Bangalore, India

Profession: Mathematician, Writer, Mental Calculator

Known As: The Human Computer

Contributions

  • Famous for her extraordinary mental calculation abilities.
  • In 1980, extracted the 23rd root of a 201-digit number in 50 seconds.
  • Set a Guinness World Record for mental calculation in 1982.
  • Authored books on mathematics, puzzles, and even astrology.
  • Wrote "The World of Homosexuals" (1977), the first Indian book on homosexuality.
  • Advocated for mental math education and empowerment through knowledge.

Legacy

  • Inspired generations with her talent and intelligence.
  • A biographical film titled Shakuntala Devi was released in 2020.
  • Recognized as one of the most brilliant mental calculators in history.
  • Continues to influence education and mathematics enthusiasts globally.
