Shakuntala Devi

Born: November 4, 1929, Bangalore, India

Died: April 21, 2013, Bangalore, India

Profession: Mental Calculator, Writer

Famous Title: "Human Computer"

Contributions

  • Performed complex mental calculations faster than computers.
  • Entered the 1982 Guinness Book of World Records for mental calculation.
  • Wrote books on mathematics, puzzles, and astrology.
  • Authored a pioneering book on homosexuality in India in 1977.
  • Promoted education, especially for women and children.

Legacy

  • Inspired generations with her extraordinary mathematical abilities.
  • A biographical film titled "Shakuntala Devi" was released in 2020 starring Vidya Balan.
  • Remembered as one of India's most brilliant minds.
